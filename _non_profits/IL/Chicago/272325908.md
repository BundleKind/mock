## Long description

To help artists develop, present and discuss their practices by providing forums for idea exchange, interdisciplinary collaboration, and experimental projects. Provide the artists community with an affordable, cooperative, and dialogue-oriented residency program, including work spaces, screen-printing studio, outdoor wood shop, art and tech facility, sound studio, fibers studio and ceramics studio.
