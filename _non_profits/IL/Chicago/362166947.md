## Long description

The mission of the american library association is to provide leadership for the development, promotion and improvement of library and information services and the profession of librarianship in order to enhance learning and ensure access to information for all. Mutual membership benefit programs, general/other-membership services provided in conjunction with member participation. Personal and organizational members totaled 54,200 as of august 31, 2015.
