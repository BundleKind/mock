## Long description

Work with local governments to advance the protection, restoration, and sustainability of the great lakes and st. Lawrence river, a resource that represents 20% of the world's surface freshwater supply and provides drinking water to over 40 million people. Active participation in 'chicago area waterway system project' to keep asian carp and other invasive species from moving between the great lakes and mississippi river watersheds and great rivers chicago project.
