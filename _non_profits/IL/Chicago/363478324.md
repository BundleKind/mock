## Long description

Bmc is called to care for the physical, social and spiritual needs of seniors and vulnerable adults of all ages in the metropolitan chicago area, with several emphases, including those of the entities of which it is the sole member, bh&mh and csg. Through the entities of which it is a member - bh&mh and csg - bmc is faithful to its non-profit, faith-based foundations and continuing commitment as a provider of quality and compassionate medical, social and spiritual services for seniors and vulnerable adults of all ages. These include independent- living services; supportive-living services; hospitality services; acute medical and ambulatory health care, including geriatric, behavioral and psychiatric services; and ancillary programs. Individualized care plans and/or social assessments are developed for each client, ensuring that services meet the range of needs common in the population addressed.
