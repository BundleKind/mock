## Long description

Develop, implement, replicate and evaluate law related education projects that impact positively on students
  
  mentor program, moot court program, federal court visits program, national & international mentor program, law enforcement academy program, we the people programs, international civics exchange program & mock trial program. In excess of 80,000 students participate. Students develop a heightened respect for the law ,the legal process and the fundamental values upon which they are based. These students have a higher attendance and academic achievement levels.
