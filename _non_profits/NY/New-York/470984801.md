## Long description

To provide crisis intervention and case management for people suffering from addiction, victims of sexual abuse and youth at risk. Amudim is an organization providing crisis intervention and case management for people suffering from addiction, victims of sexual abuse and youth at risk. During 2015, amudim provided crisis intervention and/or case management for more than 1,800 individuals or families.
